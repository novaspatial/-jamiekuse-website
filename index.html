About

Jamie Kuse is an audio engineer and producer with over two decades of industry experience. His work spans commercial releases, global broadcast, and live events — from Juno Award–winning albums to the world stage of FIFA and Valve's The International — with productions amassing over 1 billion streams worldwide.

He is the co-founder of the Emmy Award–winning NOVA Studios, one of Canada's premier Dolby Atmos facilities; NOVA Post, whose client roster spans Google, Nintendo, Valve, and Porsche; and The Immersive Preservation Project — future-proofing language and culture through archive-grade immersive recording.
