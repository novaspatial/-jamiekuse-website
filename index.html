About

Jamie Kuse is an audio engineer and producer with twenty years behind the console. His work spans commercial releases, global broadcast, and live events — from Juno Award–winning albums to the world stage of FIFA and Valve's The International — with productions amassing over 750 million streams worldwide.

He is the co-founder of the Emmy Award–winning NOVA Studios, one of Canada's premier Dolby Atmos facilities, and of The Immersive Preservation Project — future-proofing language and culture through archive-grade immersive recording.
